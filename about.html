About Us

Welcome to CrownEdge Cleaning—Manchester’s premier choice for spotless spaces and professional care. Established in 2024, our journey began with a clear mission: to deliver exceptional cleaning services that not only meet but consistently exceed expectations. From lively commercial offices to warm and welcoming homes, our expert team brings a touch of magic and meticulous care to every project. We pride ourselves on being reliable, efficient, and detail-oriented—qualities that have earned us the trust of our clients throughout Manchester.

At CrownEdge, we go beyond surface-level cleaning. Our eco-friendly methods protect your space and the environment, while our client-first approach ensures personalized service tailored to your needs. With a reputation built on quality and commitment, we’re proud to be your go-to partner for a cleaner, healthier space.

Professional Home Cleaning Service

Your home is your sanctuary—and it deserves the highest standard of care. We offer professional home cleaning solutions tailored to suit your lifestyle, schedule, and unique needs. Whether it’s a one-time deep clean, weekly maintenance, or end-of-tenancy service, our trained team handles every corner with precision, respect, and care.

Using eco-friendly products and industry-proven techniques, we leave your space spotless, fresh, and truly welcoming. From kitchens to bathrooms, bedrooms to living areas, we ensure every surface shines—so you can relax and enjoy your home, stress-free.

Whats Included
  • Complete cleaning and sanitizing of all rooms
  • Fresh bed making with clean linens (if provided)
  • Kitchen cleaning including appliances and inventory check
  • Dusting and vacuuming throughout the property
  • Window cleaning and general tidying
  • Restocking of guest amenities (if provided)
  • Property inspection and reporting of any maintenance issues
