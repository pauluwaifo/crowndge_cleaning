Your home deserves the magic of a spotless, refreshing space. At CrownEdge Cleaning, we bring professional, detail-driven cleaning right to your doorstep—so you can spend less time scrubbing and more time living.

Deep Cleaning

Perfect for spring cleans or post-event refreshes, our deep cleaning service tackles the hidden grime. We go beyond the surface to clean under furniture, inside appliances, baseboards, and those often-missed corners for a truly revitalized home.

Bedroom & Living Space Care

We make your personal spaces cozy, clean, and clutter-free. From fresh linens to polished surfaces and vacuumed floors, we turn bedrooms and lounges into serene retreats you’ll love coming home to.

Pet-Friendly Cleaning

We love pets—and we’re ready for the mess they leave behind. Our team uses safe, non-toxic products that are tough on stains and odors but gentle on your furry companions and surfaces.

Move-In / Move-Out Cleaning

Relocating? Let us handle the cleanup! Our move-in and move-out services ensure your space is spotless for its next chapter, whether you’re saying hello or goodbye to your home.

Whats Included

  • Full Apartment Dusting & Vacuuming: Thorough cleaning of all surfaces, floors, and upholstery.
  • Kitchen Cleaning: Sanitizing countertops, appliances, sinks, and cabinets.
  • Bathroom Sanitization: Deep cleaning of toilets, showers, tiles, and mirrors.
  • Linen & Towel Replacement: Fresh, hotel-quality linens and towels changed as required.
  • Waste Disposal: Emptying bins and replacing liners in all rooms.
  • Restocking Essentials: Replacing toiletries, kitchen supplies, and guest amenities.
  • Final Touches: Fluffing cushions, folding towels, and ensuring an inviting presentation.
  • Inspection Report (Optional): A post-clean inspection to ensure everything meets brand standards.
